# Business Requirement Document (BRD)

## Funnel Analytics & Conversion Optimization Framework

---

## 1. Business Objective

Improve overall conversion rate across the user journey by identifying funnel drop-offs, diagnosing user behavior, and recommending targeted optimization strategies to increase revenue efficiency.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

The platform is generating significant user traffic but failing to convert users into paying customers.

Key issues observed:

* High drop-offs across funnel stages
* Low conversion efficiency despite acquisition efforts
* Ineffective utilization of marketing spend

> As a result, the business is experiencing **revenue leakage and suboptimal return on acquisition investment**

---

## 3. Business Context

This project simulates a Business Analyst working with a product/growth team in an e-commerce environment.

* Users interact through multiple stages: browsing → cart → purchase
* Business depends on converting traffic into transactions
* Conversion efficiency directly impacts revenue and profitability

---

## 4. Stakeholders

| Stakeholder     | Objective                |
| --------------- | ------------------------ |
| Product Manager | Improve conversion rates |
| Marketing Team  | Improve campaign ROI     |
| Business Head   | Increase revenue         |
| UX/UI Team      | Reduce user friction     |

---

## 5. Scope of Analysis

### In Scope:

* Funnel stage analysis (Visit → Purchase)
* Drop-off identification
* Conversion rate calculation
* Segment-level performance (device/source/category)
* Revenue leakage estimation

### Out of Scope:

* Real-time tracking systems
* A/B testing implementation
* Backend system changes

---

## 6. Success Metrics

* Overall Conversion Rate (%)
* Stage-wise Drop-off Rate (%)
* Funnel Efficiency (%)
* Revenue per User
* Estimated Revenue Loss

---

## 7. Key Business Questions

* At which stage are users dropping the most?
* What is the conversion rate at each stage?
* Which user segments have the lowest conversion?
* What is the estimated revenue loss due to drop-offs?
* Where should the business focus to improve conversion?

---

## 8. Expected Outcome

* Clear visibility into funnel performance
* Identification of critical drop-off points
* Data-driven recommendations for conversion improvement
* Prioritized areas for business and product intervention

---

## Final Note

> This project focuses on transforming raw user behavior data into actionable business insights that directly influence conversion and revenue performance.
